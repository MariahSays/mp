Graphic Design

A Brief History

Introduction

The internet era as we currently know it is host to a multitude of trends and aesthetics,although many spur from the capabilities of technology, the styles and aesthetics we see are largely inspired by the trends of the past.
Design is all around us, it’s in what we watch, what we read, and how we learn about the world. Graphic design is a method of visual communication dating back to early forms of communication. As the world changed over time, so did how we communicate. Look at any point in time in any place and you’ll see something a little different each time.
