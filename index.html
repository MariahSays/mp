Graphic Design

A Brief History

Design is all around us, it’s in what we use,what we watch, what we read, and how we learn about the world. Graphic design, specifcally, is a method of visual communication dating back to early forms of communication. As the world changed over time, so did how we communicate. Look at any point in time in any place and you’ll see something a little different each time.
